Robin Wang, First Year DPhil Student

I'm a DPhil working with Peter along with Muhammad and Jimmy (both formerly of Peter's group). My research mainly focuses on ultrafast imaging with compressed sensing and machine learning, wakefield acceleration where I try to make them emit brilliant X-rays, and proton radiography. We've built an ultrafast camera that gives 3D data from an otherwise 2D sensor, and it's my job to test its limits, and to implement it in a wakefield experiment. We're also trying to sell the camera as a patent.

I did my undergraduate at the University of Melbourne in Physics and Spanish, and my masters on the ultrafast dynamics of things hit by XFELs (X-ray free electron lasers).
